You also need a CRR (Certificat Restreint de Radiotéléphoniste) if you want to use a VHF radio on board.

Great website for blank exams: https://www.loisirs-nautic.fr/

### CRR

- https://www.anfr.fr/gerer/radiomaritime/le-crr (78€)

Read the CRR manual, train once or twice on the ANFR plateform (https://www.anfr.fr/entrainement_crr/), then train on https://www.loisirs-nautic.fr/ then you'll be ready for the exam.
