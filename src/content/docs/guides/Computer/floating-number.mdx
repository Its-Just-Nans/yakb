## Special values

(-/+) Zero:
  - all exponent are at `0`
  - all significand are at `0`

(-/+) Infinity:
  - all exponent are at `1`
  - all significand are at `0`

NaN:
  - all exponent are at `1`
  - there is at least one `1` in the significand
