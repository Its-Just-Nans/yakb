## **Definition**

A capacitor is a passive linear dipole such that the current passing through it is proportional to the derivative of the voltage across its terminals.

- Capacitance $ C $ is measured in [Farads (F)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Farad)
- Typical range: $ [ 1 \text{pF} ; 100 \mu \text{F} ] $

## **Series Connection**

A circuit composed of multiple capacitors connected in series can be reduced to a single equivalent resistance $ C_e $, given by:

```math
\frac{1}{C_e} = \frac{1}{C_1} + \frac{1}{C_2} + \dots + \frac{1}{C_n}
```
