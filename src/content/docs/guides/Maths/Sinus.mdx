General form of a sinusoidal (sine wave) function

$$$
x(t) = m + a \cdot sin(\omega t + \phi)
$$$
$$$
x(t) = m + a \cdot sin(2 \pi f t + \phi)
$$$


Where:
- $ x(t) $: value of the function at time $ t $
- $ m $: mean value
- $ a $: amplitude of the oscillation
- $ \omega $: angular frequency (in radians per second), $ \omega = 2\pi f $
- $ f $: frequency in Hz (cycles per second)
- $ \phi $: phase shift (in radians)
