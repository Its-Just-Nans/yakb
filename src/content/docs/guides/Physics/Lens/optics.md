## Lens

### Object located at a finite distance from the lens

The object point A is to the left of F.
B′ is located at the intersection of the rays emerging from the lens: the image is real.

![Real image](./fixed_lens_real_image.svg)

The object point A is to the right of F.
B′ is located at the intersection of the extensions of the rays emerging from the lens: the image is virtual.

![Virtual image](./fixed_lens_virtual_image.svg)

### Object located at infinity

The image is real and located in the image focal plane.

- B at infinity
- A at infinity on the optical axis

![At infinity](./infinity_lens_real_image.svg)

## Laser vs LED

|                                | Laser                                       | LED                                         |
| ------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------- |
| Radiative medium, specificity? | Identical medium with radiative transitions | Identical medium with radiative transitions |
| Cavity                         | Yes                                         | No                                          |
| Coherence length               | from `cm` to `km`                           | < `cm`                                      |
| Linewidth                      | from `nm` to `pm`                           | a few `10nm`                                |
| Preferred type of emission     | Stimulated                                  | Spontaneous                                 |
| Threshold presence             | Yes                                         | No                                          |
| Quantum efficiency             | `2*10^-2`                                   | `2*10^-3`                                   |
